# FOOD-DELIVERY-CAPSTONE

📌 Project Overview
This project is a Food Delivery Data Analysis Dashboard designed to analyze customer orders, delivery performance, and overall business insights.
The goal is to transform raw data into meaningful insights that help improve customer satisfaction, delivery efficiency, and business growth.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze order patterns and customer behavior
Identify top-performing cuisines
Evaluate delivery and preparation efficiency
Track customer complaints and service quality
Build an interactive dashboard for decision-making

🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
Excel – Data cleaning & preprocessing
MySQL – Data storage & querying
Power BI – Data visualization & dashboard creation

📊 Dashboard Insights
🔢 Key Metrics
Total Orders: 100K
Total Order Value: 82M
Total Delivery Time: 5M
Average Rating: 3.76 ⭐
Total Preparation Time: 3M

🍜 Cuisine Analysis
Continental cuisine has the highest number of orders
Chinese and American cuisines are also highly preferred
Italian and Thai cuisines have comparatively lower demand

📅 Monthly Sales Trend
Orders are consistent throughout the year
Slight peaks observed in certain months indicating seasonal demand

🚚 Delivery Performance
Delivered orders dominate the dataset
Cancelled orders contribute very little but still impact efficiency

⚠️ Customer Complaints
Cold Food is the most common issue
Followed by Wrong Item and Late Delivery
Indicates improvement needed in delivery time and food handling

📊 Distribution Overview
Majority of data is driven by order count
Customer and restaurant contributions are smaller but significant

💡 Key Insights
Improving delivery speed can increase ratings
Addressing cold food complaints can enhance customer satisfaction
Focusing on popular cuisines can boost revenue
Monitoring cancellations can improve operational efficiency
